Project overview

The Lifestyle App is designed to empower senior citizens and individuals with terminal illnesses to easily document their life stories. With a user-friendly interface and a strong emphasis on privacy and security, the Lifestyle App encourages users to preserve their legacies. It fosters a sense of community and mutual support while ensuring that these personal histories endure for generations. As an ongoing project, the Lifestyle App remains committed to enhancing the experience and making life documentation accessible to all, enriching lives and celebrating unique stories.

Customer objective

Enable users to effortlessly document and share their life stories, fostering a sense of connection, reflection, and personal legacy preservation through the Lifestyle App.

Business objective

Our mission is to establish the Lifestyle App as the premier platform for life documentation, enhancing user well-being, fostering community, and preserving personal legacies for generations to come.

Some wireframing

We utilised wireframes for platform to blueprint app structure, enhance usability, streamline development, and ensure a user-centric, efficient final product.
